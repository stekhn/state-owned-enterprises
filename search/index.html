Why?

State-O aims to shed light on stated-owned enterprises and offers information in a simple searchable database. More and more public services are privatized and there are many known cases of state-owned or partially state-owned companies being involved in scandals and shady business.

Help us!

Information about publicly owned companies and public private partnerships is many times hidden in PDFs published by government bodies, city councils or other public sources. Help us by sending us data about state owned companies so we can expand State-O and build a database for SOCs all over Europe!
